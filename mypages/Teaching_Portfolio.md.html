**Xiang Huang's Sample Teaching Portfolio** This is a collection of Xiang's teaching portfolio. Created as part of the application package for a teaching assistant professor position at Caltech. I mainly teach theoretical courses such as **Discrete Structures** and **Algorithms and Theory of Computation** courses at UIS. (#) Syllabi - [CSC 302 Discrete Structures](tap/CSC302.pdf) - [CSC 482 Algorithms and Theory of Computation](tap/CSC482.pdf) I embrace the use of ChatGPT in labs for this course. Students need to write detailed reports on the lab results.
